Red Curry

A Delicous and Simple Recipe for a Thai Favorite!

This dish is easy to make and perfect for cold days or any time you're craving Thai flavors!

Spicy and rich, this recipe is served with rice and provide plenty of excellent leftovers

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion diced
  • 1 red bell pepper chopped
  • 3-5 green onion finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp cilantro chopped
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp fresh grated ginger
  • 3 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1 14 oz can of coconut milk
  • 2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 pounds chicken thighs
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 bunch broccolini cut into 3 inch chunks
  • optional ingredients:
    • mushrooms
    • potatoes
    • serrano pepper

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan of 4 cups of water, cook rice according to package instructions; set aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in large stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic cooking until fragrant, about 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add ginger, red curry paste, salt and pepper stirring until fragrant.
  4. In a seperate pan, heat cooking oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper to taste and place them in pan. Cook for 2 minutes on each side or until skin has a sear on it.
  5. Add chicken thighs and chicken broth to onion mixture, stir in cocunut milk. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a simmer.
  6. Add red pepper, broccolini and any other vegetables to stock pot and let simmer uncovered for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Add lime juice, green onion, cilantro and serrano pepper if using. Cook for another 10 minutes
  8. Serve over rice and enjoy immediately!
